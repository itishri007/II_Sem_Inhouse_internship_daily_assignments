ABOUT PRAVAH

The Heartbeat of SKIT Jaipur

PRAVAH is the flagship annual techno-cultural and sports festival of Swami Keshvanand Institute of Technology, Management & Gramothan (SKIT), Jaipur. Every year, it brings together students from across Rajasthan and India to celebrate talent, creativity, technology, sportsmanship, and innovation.

From high-energy dance battles and electrifying concerts to coding competitions, robotics challenges, photography, drama, esports, treasure hunts, and exciting sports tournaments, PRAVAH transforms the entire campus into a celebration of ideas and entertainment.
